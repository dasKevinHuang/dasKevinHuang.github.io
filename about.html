About Me

I'm Kevin Huang and I'm a member of the 2016 Squirrels cohort at Dev Bootcamp in San Francisco. Before joining Dev Bootcamp, I studied political science at UC Santa Cruz and worked on education, labor, and tech policy. I was born and raised in the San Francisco Bay Area - and I'm happy to be able to call it home!

I decided to dedicate myself to learning how to code because of how code can fuel a limitless capacity to build and create meaningful projects in the world. I have always been passionate about making the world a better place and I hope to do so through code! When I'm not learning new ways to write code, I might be exploring California, playing video games, or going out for a bike ride.

Also, I love Nintendo video games and franchises, so if you like playing Super Smash Bros. too, let's play!
